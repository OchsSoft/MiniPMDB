# Code of conduct

MiniPMDB contributors and maintainers are expected to be respectful, specific, and constructive.

Harassment, discriminatory conduct, threats, deliberate exposure of private information, and sustained personal attacks are not acceptable in project spaces. Technical disagreement is welcome; hostility toward people is not.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and comments that violate this standard and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct issues privately to the repository owner. Reports will be handled with discretion and conflicts of interest will be disclosed or delegated when practical.

This policy applies to repository discussions, pull requests, issues, and project events.
